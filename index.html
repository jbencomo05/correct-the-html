My Bigfoot Sighting

Hey there, fellow adventurers!

I have to admit, I didn’t expect my weekend hike in the Cascade Mountains to turn into something straight out of a legend. But here we are—I saw Bigfoot.

It all started early in the morning. The mist was still clinging to the trees as I set off on the trail...

I noticed a shadow in the distance—tall, dark, and… not quite right...

I tried to convince myself it was a trick of the light, but then it moved again...

In a blink, it disappeared into the woods, vanishing as quickly as it had appeared...

Some people will say I imagined it. Some will say it was a trick of the light...

If you ever find yourself hiking in the wild, keep your eyes peeled. You might just encounter the creature of legends.

Stay curious,
